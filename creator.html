Meet the Creator

Hi, I'm Samantha! I started "Crazy for Cats" because of my lifelong love for animals. I am definitely more of a cat-person, but I love all animals of all types, sizes, and looks! My goal here is to share helpful tips and clear up any common misconceptions about our feline friends. Originally, I am from New York. I like to say upstate for the people that don't know New York very well and downstate for the ones that do. I moved out here to Arizona with my dog, Jax, in 2022 and it has been the best decision of my life.


To the right, you can see the best office assistance (my cat!) and my two wonderful dogs who keep me company while I made this website.

Meet Dewey

This is Dewey. He is your typical Domestic Shorthair from the shelter. He loves to be with his people but takes a minute to get used to newcomers. Once he gets used to you though, he's a best friend for life. He's adventurous and affectionate and reminds me a lot of my childhood cat, Misty who was just an absolute angel. Dewey is definitely the "king" of the household. The dogs know not to mess with him, even Jax and he is 85 lbs! My boyfriend actually got him for me and we only knew each other for about 2 months at the time which definitely wasn't the smartest decision, but everything worked out perfectly to say the least.

Meet My Dogs

These are my dogs. Jax is the German Shepherd and he is about 5 and a half years old now. I adopted him at a year and a half prior to me moving out here to Arizona from New York. He's a great dog and unlike most shepherds, is quite a lazy guy. He's was a great addition to the family from the start, doing great around both other people and children! Now Silvia is TECHNICALLY my boyfriends dog but we adopted her together so I like to say she's mine as well. She is an absolute sweetheart, but a little more on the timid size. We think she might be a mix of a Lab Retriever and an Italian Greyhound but we're not entirely sure. We got her at 2 months and she's going on 3 in July.
