Ragdoll

Origin: California, United States

Temperament: Loving, gentle, calm, and sociable.

Ragdolls are the forever kids of the cat world. They don't really tend to grow up too quick, if at all. This cat has a wonderful and laid back personality where you can really see their sweetness shine through. Although always young in mind, they are an incredibly trainable breed and can be taught to fetch and come when called. Some owners say that their Ragdolls are attuned to their people's emotional needs, making them great supportive companions. They are a moderately active breed, appreciating play sessions but not very insistent upon them. These are a wonderful breed that make great pets to have along with children, or other cats and dogs.


These are a larger breed of cat with a long coat that surprisingly doesn't require a crazy amount of time. Regular combing at least once or twice a week with a steel comb can help find and remove any loose hairs or tangles. Ragdolls have the colorpoint gene, meaning that the body is always lighter in color than the head, legs and tail. Three of the four Ragdoll paterrns also have the bicolor gene which can spice up the pointed marking with dramatic white acccents.
