Adoptable Cat Resources

If you feel like you're ready to find your new companion, start your search with these reputable Arizona and national organizations.


It's important to note that some places will require home visits! Nothing wrong with a home visit, they just want to make sure the kitten is going into a good environment. However, if you're not ok with a home visit then the shelter may not adopt out to you. Just something to keep in mind.


Now, if you're more of a breeder person I am a firm believer in "Adopt or Shop RESPONSIBLY" so make sure you do your own research on a breeder you choose to go with to make sure you're supporting good people who health test and take good care of their animals! (This applies to dogs as well.)
